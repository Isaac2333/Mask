作品简介

Introduction

       这是一个关于卸下伪装，与人真诚社交的故事。人说吃一堑长一智，每次受伤都会带上一个面具。鲁迅说：“面具戴久了，就会长到脸上，再想揭下来，除非伤筋动骨扒皮。”我们的故事讲述了一个小三角由于恐惧带上了面具，却引来其他面具的追击，在混乱中，小三角揭下了一个人的面具，发现了背后藏着的另一个小三角。我们希望通过这个故事让人们思考社交当中真实的重要性。

          This is a story about taking off the disguise and socializing sincerely with people. People say that every time you get hurt, you will wear a mask. Lu Xun said: "if you wear a mask for a long time, you will grow to your face. If you want to take it off again, you will have to hurt your muscles and bones and peel off your skin." Our story tells the story of a little triangle wearing a mask because of fear, but it leads to the pursuit of other masks. In the chaos, the little triangle takes off a person's mask and discovers another triangle hidden behind it. We want to use this story to make people think about the real importance of social interaction.
